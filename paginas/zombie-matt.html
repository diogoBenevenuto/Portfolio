Zombie Matt


In a post-apocalyptic world, zombies wander through the ruins, each with a challenging calculation in their heads. Your mission, as a fearless zombie hunter, is to defeat them using the power of mathematics! Explore scenarios filled with problems and choose the right answers to eliminate these creatures.
