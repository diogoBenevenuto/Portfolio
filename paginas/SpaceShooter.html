Star Wars space shooter


Space Shooter is a classic spaceship game where the action never stops! Destroy enemy ships, rack up points, and test your reflexes as waves of enemies keep getting bigger and more challenging. The more you take down, the tougher it gets! A simple yet addictive game, developed in JavaScript as part of an academic project. Can you survive the chaos of space?
