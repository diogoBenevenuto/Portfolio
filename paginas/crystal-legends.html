Crystal Legends


Crystal Legenda is a 3D RPG with 2D elements that offers a nostalgic experience, bringing the feel of classic games. The game combines the charm of 2D graphics with the depth of a 3D world, creating a unique immersion for players. In Crystal Legenda, you'll dive into a rich and detailed universe where combat is turn-based, allowing for careful and engaging strategy. Each battle is an opportunity to explore tactics and enhance your skills as you uncover mysteries and face challenges on an epic journey. Get ready to relive the magic of old-school games with a fresh perspective, and discover a world where tradition meets innovation.

Project Information

  • Type of Project: Academic Project
  • Tech: Smile Game Builder
  • Platform: PC
  • Role: Programmer and Designer
