About Me

Hello, I'm Diogo, a developer passionate about technology and design. With experience in web development, game creation, and digital design, I have extensive knowledge in technologies such as JavaScript, Python, C#, C++, HTML, CSS, Node.js, and game development tools like Unreal Engine, Unity, Smile Game Builder, Bakin RPG Developer, and Construct.

I’m always seeking new challenges to enhance my skills and create innovative projects. I work on indie game projects and backend solutions, and I continually stay updated with the latest industry trends and best practices.

Outside of work, I’m a gaming enthusiast who enjoys watching videos and streams for inspiration on new projects. I love watching series, practicing boxing to relax and stay active, and exploring outdoor activities like hiking, walking, and playing basketball. I’m also a fan of reading, which helps fuel my creativity.

If you're looking for someone with a unique blend of technical and creative skills, I’m here to help. Let’s turn your ideas into reality!
